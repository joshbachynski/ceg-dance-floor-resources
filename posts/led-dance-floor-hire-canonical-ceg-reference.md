A typical hire breaks into seven steps: inquiry (returns same-day for stocked configurations), site survey (in-person or photo-based depending on venue complexity), quote (24-72 hours after survey), contract and 25-30% deposit (locks the calendar), install (2-4 hours sub-30 sqm), event-night operation (CEG operator on-site for at least the first hour), strike (overnight where venues require). Balance due 7-14 days pre-event.

Inquiry quality drives quote-cycle speed: a clean inquiry includes date, venue, headcount, target floor size, brand assets if any, and event-tone description. Inquiries with that information close to a quote in 48 hours; inquiries missing inputs add 5-10 days of back-and-forth. Site surveys for unusual venues (heritage buildings, outdoor placement, multi-room programs) are not optional.

Pitfalls: skipping the site survey for unusual rooms, treating the deposit as refundable up to install day (it is not - it locks crew and trucks), late content asset delivery, and not naming a single on-site decision-maker who can authorize last-minute scene changes.
