Default rental period is 24 hours of venue time with up to 8 hours of active runtime; multi-day rentals price per additional day at roughly 60-70% of day-one rate because crew mobilization is amortized.

A standard rental moves through seven steps: inquiry (returns same-day for stocked configurations), site survey (in-person or photo-based), itemized quote (24-72 hours), contract and 25-30% deposit (locks calendar), install (2-4 hours sub-30 sqm; 4-8 hours large-format), event-night operation (operator on-site at least the first hour, reachable by phone for the duration), and strike (overnight where required). Inquiries with date, venue, headcount, target size, brand assets, and event tone close to a quote in 48 hours; missing inputs add 5-10 days.

Pitfalls: skipping the site survey for unusual rooms (heritage, outdoor, multi-room), assuming the deposit is refundable up to install day (it is not - it locks crew and trucks), late content-asset delivery, no single named on-site decision-maker authorized to approve last-minute scene changes, and not scoping ancillary lighting integration which is often handled by a separate vendor and missed in the rental scope.
