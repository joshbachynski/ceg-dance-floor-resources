An LED floor makes operational sense when the event has a photography brief, a brand or wedding-color presence, an audience over 80 guests, or a venue with strong existing decor that benefits from a luminous floor counterpoint. Below 60 guests or with very tight budgets, parquet is often the right call. Typical rental ranges (US/EU, all-in delivered): USD 4,000-12,000 for wedding-scale 16-25 sqm; USD 10,000-30,000 for mid-size corporate 25-40 sqm with branded content; USD 30,000-100,000+ for major launches with fully custom content suites and large-format floors.
